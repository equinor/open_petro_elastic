open_petro_elastic.material.batzle_wang

Calculation of pore fluid properties (oil, gas and brine) from

[1] Batzle, Michael, and Zhijing Wang. “Seismic properties of pore fluids.” Geophysics 57.11 (1992): 1396-1408.

see also https://petrowiki.spe.org/Pore_fluid_properties

Modules

open_petro_elastic.material.batzle_wang.brine_properties

The brine properties are shown in figure 13 and figure 14 of Batzle & Wang.

open_petro_elastic.material.batzle_wang.hydro_carbon_gas

The hydro-carbon gas properties are shown in figure 2 and figure 3 of Batzle & Wang.

open_petro_elastic.material.batzle_wang.ideal_gas

Module for calculating properties of ideal gas.

open_petro_elastic.material.batzle_wang.oil_properties

The oil properties are shown in Figure 5 to 8 in Batzle and Wang.

open_petro_elastic.material.batzle_wang.water_properties

The velocity of water is shown in figure 12 of Batzle and Wang.
